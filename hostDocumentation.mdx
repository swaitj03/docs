## Overview

WitsEats is a web-based food ordering and delivery platform that supports:

- Food browsing and ordering for **buyers**
- Order fulfilment and communication for **drivers**
- Restaurant and user management for **admins**

Each role has its own interface and set of features tailored to their responsibilities.

## Placing an Order (Buyers)

### Browse Restaurants

- On the homepage, scroll through available restaurants.
- Click on a restaurant card to view its menu.

### Add Items to Cart

- Click **Add to Cart** underneath to each menu item.
- Update quantities directly in the cart.

### Checkout

1. Click **Checkout**.
2. Fill in **delivery address** .
3. Click **Place Order**.

### Track Orders

- Real-time order status updates:
  - `Order Received`
  - `Being Prepared`
  - `Out for Delivery`
  - `Delivered`

## Restaurant Management (Admins)

### Add New Restaurant

1. Go to the **Admin Dashboard**.
2. Click **Add Restaurant**.
3. Enter:
   - Restaurant name
   - Location
   - Menu details
4. Click **Save**.

### Edit Existing Restaurant

1. Navigate to the **Restaurants** section.
2. Select a restaurant.
3. Update its details.
4. Click **Save**.

### Approve Drivers

1. Open the **Driver Approval** section.
2. Review pending driver applications.
3. Click **Approve**.
4. Drivers are notified via email.

---

## Order Fulfilment (Drivers)

### View Assigned Orders

- Log in to the Driver Dashboard to see a list of current orders.

### Accept Orders

1. Click **Accept** on a new order.
2. View restaurant and buyer info.

### Complete Delivery

1. Navigate to **Deliveries**.
2. Click **Mark as Delivered**.

### Communication

- Use the **built-in chat** to contact the buyer.
